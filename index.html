Die Hard: With a Vengeance

Summary: John McClane teams up with Zeus Carver to stop a vengeful terrorist, Simon Gruber, who orchestrates a series of deadly challenges across New York City. The movie combines high-stakes action with a gripping cat-and-mouse game.

Genre: Action, Thriller

Director: John McTiernan

Stars: Bruce Willis, Samuel L. Jackson, Jeremy Irons

Key Themes: Revenge, partnership, urban terrorism.

The Da Vinci Code

Summary: Symbologist Robert Langdon and cryptologist Sophie Neveu unravel a murder mystery connected to a hidden secret guarded by the Catholic Church. Their journey takes them through historical landmarks in Europe, uncovering clues tied to Leonardo da Vinci's works.

Genre: Mystery, Thriller

Director: Ron Howard

Stars: Tom Hanks, Audrey Tautou, Ian McKellen

Key Themes: Religion, conspiracy, hidden knowledge.
